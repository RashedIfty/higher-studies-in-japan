Why Japan?

Why Choose Japan for Higher Studies?

01

World-Class Education

Top-performing OECD nation in science and math, with globally ranked universities and cutting-edge research.

02

Research Powerhouse

The world's 3rd-largest R&D spender, leading robotics, AI, materials, and engineering.

03

Affordable and Funded

Low tuition versus the West, plus generous scholarships like MEXT: full tuition, stipend, and airfare.

04

English-Taught Degrees

700+ full degree programs taught in English. No Japanese required to begin your studies.

05

Safe and High Quality of Life

One of the safest countries on Earth, with excellent healthcare, transport, and living standards.

06

Strong Career Pathways

Labour shortages in tech and engineering, plus simplified post-study work visas for graduates.

408,000+ international students chose Japan in 2025, a record high.

MEXT Scholarship

Two Ways to Apply: Choose Your Track

Track 01

Embassy Recommendation

Apply through your local Japanese Embassy

  • Screened by Japanese Embassy in your country
  • Admission No university acceptance needed first
  • Selection Written exams plus interview
  • Slots More slots, but high competition
  • Placement MEXT decides your final university
Track 02

University Recommendation

Apply directly to a Japanese University

  • Screened by The university's own office
  • Admission University nominates you directly
  • Selection No central exam, internal review
  • Slots Limited quota per university
  • Placement Guaranteed at your chosen university

Both offer the same full funding. We will walk through each track next.

Before You Apply

Eligibility & Required Documents

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Under 35 for Master's / PhD (born on or after Apr 2, 1992)
  • Nationality A country with diplomatic ties to Japan; not Japanese
  • Degree Relevant Bachelor's (or Master's) completed or expected
  • Academics Strong record, typically GPA 3.5+ / 4.0 or 70%+
  • Language English proof (IELTS / TOEFL) or Japanese ability
  • Health Good health, certified by a doctor

The Most Important Step

Find Your Lab & Reach the Professor

Choosing the Right Lab

  • Fit Match your research interest to the lab's recent work
  • Supervisor Runs their own lab and holds your target degree
  • Output Read 2 to 3 of their latest papers
  • Funding Check active projects and lab resources
  • Source Use the graduate school's faculty list

Emailing the Professor

  • Timing Reach out 8 to 12 months before the deadline
  • Short Keep it under 250 words, clear and specific
  • Hook Cite 2 recent papers, link them to your idea
  • You Degree, university, and your best 2 highlights
  • Attach One CV plus one paper or research summary
  • Follow up One polite reminder after two weeks

A tailored email that shows genuine research fit puts you in the top 5% professors receive.

Stand Out From the Crowd

Tips to Strengthen Your Application

01

The Research Plan is King

It is the single most important document. Invest the most time here.

02

Be Specific, Not Generic

Name the exact question, method, and data. Vague topics get rejected.

03

Align Three Things

Your background, the professor's lab, and Japan's research strengths.

04

Show Value to Japan

Explain how your work benefits Japan's priorities and society.

05

Write Clearly

Embassy staff read it first, not experts. Avoid heavy jargon.

06

Start Early, Get Feedback

Have a MEXT scholar or professor review it before you submit.

One hour of expert feedback can be the difference between rejection and a flight to Japan.
